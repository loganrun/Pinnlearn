Enrichment

We believe that every child is blessed with unique gifts and talents. One of our goals is to help them discover these gifts and nurture and support them as the child matures into a productive member of our community.

Therefore, in addition to providing strong academic support to our participants, we also look for oportunities to expose them to a variety of enrichment activities. While there is a great need to encourage miniorities and women to pursue fields related to STEM (science, technology, engineering and math) education, we find it equally important that children be allowed to explore their creative side as well. Fortunately, there are many ways to combine these two activities while teaching children practical skills that they will need in the lives.

We are currently developing programs that will teach children how to code and develop websites, as well as powerful web applications. Additionally, we will be able to provide students with the opportunity to use programming techniques to produce graphic art design and animated movies. Please check later as these programs come online.
