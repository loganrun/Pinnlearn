Tutoring

Lets face it. School is hard and sometimes we all need help grasping new concepts. There is also no question that tutoring, one on one or in small groups, is the most effective way of teaching dicffucult topics. At Pinnacle, we offer our students access to the finest tutors. Every tutor has been carefullly screened (background checks) and rated for their understanding of the subject matter as well as their ability to communicate this informtion to their students. Many of our tutors are currently teachers and college students who are studying education.

Pinnacle Learning leverages the power of the internet to provide our students with tutoring in either an indivudual or small group basis. In this setting, the student will be able to get their specific questions answered and their needs addressed. The tutor will also be able to provide or direct the student to additional resources that can aide in understanding. Access to high quality tutoring may be the single most important factor in insuring academic success for our students. Research has continually demonstrated the importance of one on one or small group teaching.
