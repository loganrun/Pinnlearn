About Us


  • Who We Are

    We are a group of educators, business and community leaders who are dedicted to helping all children live up to their academic potential. We want to encourage our students to achieve academic excellence and rise to the forefront of the new global competitive stage.


  • OUR MISSION

    Our mision is to provide education to children and youth regardless of their geographical location. Through continued research of educational programs, we identify and implement solutions impacting the development of children and youth. In collaborate with organizations that serve children, our vision is to develop independent citizens, improving the quality of life for future generations.


  • WHAT WE BELIEVE

    We believe that every child has the ability to learn and perform at a high academic level. However, the ravages of poverty and complacency have deeply eroded our pblic schools and created a seemingly insurmountable barrier between our children and the realization of their dreams. We are committed to breaking through these barriers and giving all children access to a world class education that can propel them to future success.


  • WHAT WE DO

    We use rich media content and "gamification" to engage students to learn. This is followed by comprhensive testing to determine the student's understanding of the informtion. Finally, we use targeted tutoring to address areas of student weaknesss and any knowledge gaps. This three prong approach is ideal in helping our students make significant improvement in their academic achievement.
