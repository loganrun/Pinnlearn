Academic Support

We believe that every child has the ability to learn and perform at a high academic level. But school can be diffucult at times and even the brightest student can struggle in a particular academic subject. Given this reality, coupled with the current state of over crowded classrooms, it is no wonder how a student can fall behind and not live up to her or his full potential.

Pinnacle Learning steps into this gap by providing strong academic support to our students. The hallmark of our programs are our highly engaging instructional videos and software which have been designed to make diffucult subjects as simple as possible. These applications, coupled with a suite of examples and practice questions allows the student to continue working on a subject until they have gained mastery over it. All of these supplemental services are web based and therefore available to assist our students according to their needs and schedules.

We are currently developing programs and curriculum for children in grades K-5. Please check back later as these programs come online.
